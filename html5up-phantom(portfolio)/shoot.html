ShootEmUp Game

ShootEmUps is also a classic arcade game,but in this case, I did a little bit of twist on it, Imagine cocktail,the classic recipe of a Negroni remains unchanged but bartenders can make changes to it for better taste. The player character aims by mouse cursor, which means it can shoot at 360 degrees, and I made a 'bomb' similar to Touhou project, whenever the bomb is used,all enemies and enemy bullets explode(expect boss enemies).
